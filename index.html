📈 Trimester Modifications
✅ Now — 20 Weeks
Full program as written. All lunges safe with modifications. Monitor balance, exhale on all efforts.
⚠️ 20–28 Weeks
Reduce KB weight as needed. More wall support in barre. Widen stances. Reduce lunge depth.
⚠️ 28–34 Weeks
Remove Sunday double. Reduce KB to 2x/week. Skip Bulgarian. All standing or all-fours.
🎯 34–36 Weeks
Yoga/barre/pilates + walking + swimming only. Very light KB. Deep squat holds priority.
🎯 36–40 Weeks
Walking + swimming + deep squat holds + pelvic floor breathing. Rest as much as needed.

📋 Golden Rules & Safety
🌟 Always Apply
💨
Exhale on every exertion
Every single rep — non-negotiable for managing intra-abdominal pressure
🚫
Never hold your breath
Instant IAP spike — always avoid Valsalva maneuver
👀
Watch for doming/coning
Stop immediately if midline ridges or cones during any exercise
💧
Hydrate between every block
Drink water — your plasma volume has increased significantly
🚨 Stop Immediately If
⬇️ Downward pelvic pressure
🔺 Midline doming or coning
😣 Sharp or pinching pain
💧 Any leaking — consult PT immediately
